# Present Continuous
    * use:
        * when we talk about something that is happening at the time of speaking.
        * when we talk about something that is happening around the time of speaking.
        * when we talk about a period around the present. Ej. Today, this week, this season, etc.
        * when we talk about changing situations.

# Simple present
    * use:
        * when we talk about things in general.
        * Things that happen all the time or repeatedly.
        * something it is true in general.
        * when say how often we do things

    * Rules: add an "s" for "he, she / it"

    * Questions: Do / Does

    * Negative sentences: do not  / does not
